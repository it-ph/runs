HOW IT WORKS

WHAT WILL I DO TO PARTICIPATE?

Register an account, track and record your run & ride sessions using Strava, then take a screenshot of your session and upload it on your RunProud.Personiv.com account.t

REGISTRATION PERIOD

July 1, 2019 - June 31, 2019

RUN PERIOD

August 1, 2019 - August 31, 2019

REGISTRATION PROCEDURE

Registration payment will be handled per site and can be via salary deduction following the site's agreed terms (e.g. one payment, 2 cutoffs, etc). Register and create an account by clicking the REGISTER button at the start of the RunProud website. Update your profile and race preferences in the website.

REGISTRATION FEE

Registration fee will be dependent per site. Announcements will be made per facility. Fee is inclusive of a finisher shirt/s, medal/s, and freebie of the month.

WHAT RUNNING APPLICATIONS SHOULD I USE FOR LOGGING MY RUN?

We highly encourage you to use Strava. This will allow us to monitor your progress for both run & ride in one app. Road, Trail and Indoor activities are accepted as long as the run session is recorded (upload screenshot). Indoor includes running on a treadmill and biking using a stationary bike.

HOW DO I SUBMIT MY DAILY/WEEKLY RUN PROOF?

You need to register with an email (personal or company email) and password, and you can submit your run sessions anytime by logging in on our web app. Screenshot should display duration, distance (in kilometers), time, and date.

WHEN CAN I RECEIVE MY ENTITLEMENTS?

Entitlements can be received 15 to 30 days after the race ends. The entitlements will be handled per site.
