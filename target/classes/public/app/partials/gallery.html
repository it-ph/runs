HOW IT WORKS

WHAT WILL I DO TO PARTICIPATE?

Register an account, track and record your run or ride session; take a screenshot of your session and upload it on your RunProud.Personiv.com account together with a selfie taken after each activity. Uploads should be done weekly on or before Monday of the following week for the submission to be approved.

REGISTRATION PERIOD

August 1 to August 31, 2019

RUN PERIOD

Setp 1, 2019 to Sept 30, 2019

REGISTRATION PROCEDURE

Registration payment will be handled per site and can be via salary deduction following the site's agreed terms (e.g. one payment, 2 cutoffs, etc). Register and create an account by clicking the REGISTER button at the start of the RunProud website. Update your profile and race preferences in the website.

REGISTRATION FEE

Registration fee will be dependent per site. Announcements will be made per facility. Fee is inclusive of a finisher shirt/s and other entitlements approved by your facility. One registration fee for any activity (Run Proud only, Ride Proud only or Duathlon) and no refunds allowed.

WHAT RUNNING APPLICATIONS SHOULD I USE FOR LOGGING MY RUN?

We are requiring all participants to use an app that can track running and biking like Strava. This will allow us to monitor your progress for both run & ride in one app. Road, Trail and Indoor activities are accepted as long as the run & ride session is recorded (upload a photo of the distance recorded in the treadmill or stationary bike).

HOW DO I SUBMIT MY DAILY/WEEKLY RUN PROOF?

You need to register with an email (personal or company email) and password, and you can submit your run sessions on a weekly basis by logging in on our web app. Screenshot should display duration, distance (in kilometers), time, and date together with a selfie after each activity.

WHEN CAN I RECEIVE MY ENTITLEMENTS?

Entitlements can be received 15 to 30 days after the race ends. The entitlements will be handled per site.

WHAT ARE THE RUN PROUD REQUIREMENTS?

Run 160 KM within the month of Sept 2019 and get a Run Proud T-Shirt plus other entitlements per facility

WHAT ARE THE RIDE PROUD REQUIREMENTS?

Bike 300 KM within the month of Sept 2019 and get a Ride Proud T-Shirt plus other entitlements per facility

WHAT ARE THE PERSONIV VIRTUAL DUATHLON REQUIREMENTS?

1. Run a minimum of 40KM by Sept 16

2. Run 160 KM by end of Sept 2019

3. Bike 300 KM within the month of Sept 2019

To get a Run Proud, Ride Proud and Personiv Duathlon T-Shirts plus other entitlements per facility
